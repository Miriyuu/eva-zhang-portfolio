Expresso Yourself
CMCI Winter Festival 2024 · Graphic Design & Media
Graphic Designer & Media Officer · Procreate · Social Media

A group project developed for the CMCI Winter Festival, where each team designed and operated a live event. "Expresso Yourself" was built around coffee culture as a medium for expression and social connection. The event was structured into multiple time-based segments, each offering different interactive activities for participants.

As the Graphic Designer and Media Officer, I was responsible for the visual identity and digital communication of the event. I designed promotional materials for social media, developed visual assets, and managed content publishing leading up to the festival.

During the event, I also handled real-time documentation and posting, capturing moments and translating the live atmosphere into digital content.

2096
Interactive Story Game · Twine 2
Independent Creator · Twine 2 · Narrative Design

"2096" is an interactive story game set in a speculative future city, developed using Twine 2. The narrative draws inspiration from anti-utopian works such as "No.6" and "1984", constructing a world where society is divided by age, labour capacity, and class.

Through branching storylines and player choices, the game invites users to navigate this system and reflect on the structures that govern it. The design explores how interactive storytelling can be used to engage with political and social themes.

This project combines narrative design, world-building, and basic coding within Twine, experimenting with how digital formats can extend theoretical ideas into experiential forms.
